## 🎓 Eligibility Criteria

To apply for UG or PG programs at UPES Online Online, you must meet the following criteria:

* **Undergraduate Programs (BBA, BCA, B.Com):** Passed 10+2 (Higher Secondary) from a recognized board (CBSE, ICSE, or State boards) with a minimum of 45-50% aggregate marks.
* **Postgraduate Programs (MBA, MCA, M.Com):** Graduated with a 3-year bachelor's degree from a UGC-recognized university with at least 50% marks (45% for reserved categories).

## 📝 Step-by-Step Admission Process

### Step 1: Online Registration
Visit the official application portal or coordinate with a verified academic counselor. Register using your email address and active mobile number to create your credentials.

### Step 2: Form Submission
Log in and fill in your personal, contact, and academic details. Carefully choose your desired program and specialization.

### Step 3: Upload Documents
Upload your scanned certificates and ID proof as per the specified dimensions. Ensure text is clear and readable.

### Step 4: Academic Review
The university admissions team will verify your uploaded documents. This process usually takes 24 to 48 hours.

### Step 5: Fee Payment
Once verified, you will receive a payment link. Pay the registration/semester fee using credit/debit cards, Net Banking, or select an EMI option.

### Step 6: Admission Confirmation
On successful payment, you will receive an official confirmation mail with your enrollment number and access credentials for the LMS platform.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. How long does the online verification process take?**
Document verification typically takes 1 to 2 business days. If any document is unclear, the admissions desk will contact you to re-upload.

**Q2. Can I get a refund if I cancel my admission?**
Yes. Refunds are processed according to the UGC Refund Policy guidelines, which specify deduction rates based on the cancellation date relative to the official batch start.

**Q3. Is there a physical campus visit required during admission?**
No, the entire process from form submission to document verification and fee payment is conducted online.
