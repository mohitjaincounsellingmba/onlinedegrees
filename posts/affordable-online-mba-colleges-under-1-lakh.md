## How to Evaluate
1. **Accreditation** – Must be **UGC approved**.
2. **Placement Record** – Look for active career services, internship portals.
3. **Curriculum Relevance** – Check for specialization options aligned with industry demand.
4. **Student Reviews** – Search forums for feedback on teaching quality and support.

## Tips to Reduce Costs Further
- **Scholarships**: Many state universities offer merit‑based scholarships up to 30%.
- **EMI Plans**: Use 0% EMI options offered by partner banks.
- **Corporate Sponsorship**: If you are employed, ask your employer for tuition assistance.
