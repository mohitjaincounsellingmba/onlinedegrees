**Q1. Is JAIN University online MBA valid in India?**
Yes. JAIN is a UGC-DEB approved Deemed University with NAAC A++ accreditation. Its online degree is equivalent to a regular MBA for all government and private sector purposes.

**Q2. What makes JAIN Online MBA unique compared to others?**
