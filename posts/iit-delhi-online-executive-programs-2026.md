## **IIT Delhi Online Fees 2026**

The fee structure for executive programs at IIT Delhi is designed to be affordable for professionals, with many courses offering **EMI options** and education loan support.

*   **Certificate Programs:** Usually range between **₹1.00 Lakh to ₹2.50 Lakhs**.
*   **Executive PG Diplomas:** Can vary between **₹3.00 Lakhs to ₹6.00 Lakhs** depending on the duration and depth of the curriculum.
*   **EMI Support:** Most programs are available with monthly EMI options, making them accessible to a wider range of working professionals.

## **Placement Assistance and Career Impact**

One of the biggest advantages of an IIT Delhi tag is the employer trust it commands. While online programs differ from full-time residential courses, IIT Delhi provides significant placement support:

*   **IIT Delhi Brand Value:** Mentioning an IIT Delhi executive program on your resume significantly increases your visibility among recruiters.

*   **Alumni Network:** Graduates become part of the massive IIT Delhi alumni network, opening doors to high-level leadership roles in India and abroad.

## **Eligibility and Admission Process 2026**

1.  **Educational Background:** Most executive programs require a Graduate degree (B.Tech, B.Com, BBA, B.Sc) with at least 50% marks.
2.  **Work Experience:** A minimum of **1 to 3 years of work experience** is typically mandatory for executive courses.
3.  **Selection:** Unlike JEE or GATE, selection for executive programs is often based on your **profile, work history, and a personal interview**. Some advanced programs may require a short entrance screening test.
