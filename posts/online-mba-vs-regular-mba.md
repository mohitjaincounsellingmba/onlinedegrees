# Online MBA vs Regular MBA – Which is Better?

## 1. Flexibility
- **Online MBA** – Study from anywhere, asynchronous lectures, can keep full‑time job.
- **Regular MBA** – Fixed class schedule, on‑campus attendance required.

## 2. Cost
| Program | Tuition (₹) | Total Cost (incl. living) |
|---------|-------------|--------------------------|
| Online MBA (top Indian schools) | 80‑150 k | 2‑3 Lakh |
| Regular MBA (IIM, XLRI, etc.) | 300‑600 k | 8‑12 Lakh |

## 3. Duration
- **Online MBA** – 1.5‑2 years, often self‑paced.
- **Regular MBA** – Typically 2 years full‑time.

## 4. Networking & Campus Experience
- **Online** – Virtual cohorts, global peer group, limited face‑to‑face interaction.
- **Regular** – Strong alumni network, on‑campus events, corporate visits.

## 5. ROI & Salary Impact
| Program | Avg. Salary Increase (%) |
|---------|--------------------------|
| Online MBA | 30‑40 |
| Regular MBA | 40‑55 |

## Verdict
If you need **flexibility**, **lower cost**, and can leverage **virtual networking**, the **online MBA** is the better choice in 2026. For those seeking a **traditional campus experience** and **higher immediate salary boost**, a **regular MBA** remains compelling.
