## Why NMAT is Completely Different

For many CAT aspirants, NMAT comes as a shock. Why? 
1. **Adaptive Algorithm:** NMAT adapts to your performance. If you answer a question correctly, the next one is harder (and carries more weight). If you answer incorrectly, it gets easier.
2. **Zero Negative Marking:** There are absolutely no penalties for wrong answers. Hence, strategy is EVERYTHING, and you should never leave a question unattempted.
3. **Strict Sectional Timing:** The 120 minutes are not yours to split as you wish. 

Practicing these elements on paper is simply impossible. You need a digital tool that simulates these exact constraints.
