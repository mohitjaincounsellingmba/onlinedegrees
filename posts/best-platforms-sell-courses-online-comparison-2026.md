## 🏛️ 1. The Marketplace Route (Udemy, Skillshare)

Marketplaces are great for beginners who have no audience.
*   **Pros:** They handle the marketing; zero upfront cost.
*   **Cons:** They control the pricing (frequent $9 sales), take a massive revenue cut, and prevent you from directly messaging your students. 
*   **Verdict:** Good for "Side Income," but not for building a "Business."

---

## 🚀 2. The Standard LMS Route (Teachable, Thinkific, Kajabi)

These are "Self-Hosted" solutions where you bring your own audience.
*   **Pros:** Better control over branding and pricing than marketplaces.
*   **Cons:** High monthly fees ($99 - $399/month), and students still view your content through a browser or a generic "LMS App" which feels less professional.

---

## 🏗️ 3. The Branded App Revolution (The 2026 Winner)

In 2026, high-growth educators are moving to **Branded White-Label Apps**. 
*   **Why it works:** Your logo is on the student's phone. You send targeted push notifications. You provide secure, ad-free learning. 
*   **Revenue Impact:** Without a platform cut, your profit margins increase significantly. More importantly, you build an **Asset**, not just a course.

---

## 📑 4. How to Choose the Right Platform?

1.  **If you have 0 followers:** Start with YouTube and a Marketplace.
2.  **If you have 5k+ followers:** Launch your own website/LMS.
3.  **If you want to scale to 10k+ paid students:** You MUST have your own **Branded Mobile App**.
