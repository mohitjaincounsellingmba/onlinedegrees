## Online MBA vs. Distance MBA: What is the Difference?

Before looking at the universities, it is important to understand the difference between the two models:

*   **Distance (ODL) MBA:** Study materials are sent by post or collected from study centers. Classes are held on weekends at physical regional centers, and examinations are written offline on paper.
*   **Online MBA:** The entire learning process is digital. Classes are held live or via recorded modules on a Learning Management System (LMS), assignments are submitted online, and examinations are conducted via online proctored systems from home. There is zero physical travel required.

### Is an online MBA accepted by top private employers?
Yes. According to current government regulations, online degrees from UGC-DEB approved universities are recognized equally with regular on-campus degrees for employment in corporate sectors.
