The **Common University Entrance Test (CUET)** has become the gateway to undergraduate admissions in top Central and State universities across India. Introduced by the National Testing Agency (NTA), CUET is now mandatory for admission to BBA and BCA programs in over 280+ participating universities.

Whether you're targeting a **BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)** for a management career or a **BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)** to enter the tech industry, this guide covers the **best CUET colleges for 2026 admissions**.

---

## What is CUET and Why Does It Matter?

CUET replaced Class 12 percentage as the sole admission criterion for central universities. Here is what you need to know:

- **Conducted by:** National Testing Agency (NTA)
- **Mode:** Computer-Based Test (CBT) — Online
- **Sections:** Domain-specific subjects (Accountancy, Business Studies, Maths), General Aptitude, and Language
- **Score Validity:** Valid for one academic year only
- **Participating Institutions:** 280+ universities across India

## CUET BBA/BCA Exam: Subject Combinations

To appear for BBA or BCA via CUET, you must select the right combination of domain subjects:

**For BBA:**
- Accountancy / Business Studies + Economics / Maths
- General Test (Aptitude, Reasoning, General Knowledge)

**For BCA:**
- Mathematics + Computer Science / Informatics Practices
- General Test (Aptitude, Reasoning)

## FAQs

### Is CUET mandatory for all BBA and BCA colleges?
No, CUET is currently mandatory only for colleges that officially participate in the CUET system. Many private universities like Symbiosis, Manipal, and VIT hold their own separate entrance exams alongside CUET.

### What is a good CUET score for top BBA colleges?
For the most prestigious CUET BBA colleges like Delhi University, you need 90 to 95+ percentile. For mid-tier good private colleges like Chandigarh University and LPU, a 55 to 70 percentile is typically sufficient for admission.

### Can I get an online BBA if I miss the CUET cutoff?
Yes! Several UGC-approved universities offer fully online BBA programs without requiring CUET scores, including Amity University Online, Jain University Online, and LPU Online. These are especially valuable for those who want to work while studying.
