## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. Is SMU online MBA valid for government jobs?**
Yes. SMU is UGC-entitled and NAAC A+ accredited, making its degree fully valid for government job applications and PSU recruitment.

**Q2. What is the difference between SMU DDE MBA and SMU Online MBA?**
SMU DDE (Distance Education) is the older correspondence-based model. The newer online program uses a digital LMS (via the Online Manipal platform) with live sessions and digital content. Both are from the same institution.

**Q3. Is SMU the same as Manipal MAHE?**
No. SMU (Sikkim campus) and MAHE (Manipal, Karnataka) are different institutions under the Manipal Group. MAHE holds NAAC A++ and a QS ranking — making its degree more premium. SMU holds NAAC A+.

**Q4. Why is SMU MBA so cheap compared to others?**
SMU has maintained affordable fees as part of its mission to make quality education accessible. The trade-off is less placement support and lower corporate brand recognition compared to NMIMS or Manipal MAHE.

**Q5. Can I do dual specialization in SMU online MBA?**
Yes — and this is actually included in the base fee, which is rare. You can combine Finance + Marketing, HR + Finance, etc.
