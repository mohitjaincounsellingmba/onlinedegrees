## Step-by-Step Guide: How to Check CUET UG Result 2026

Once NTA officially releases the results, you can access your scorecard directly on the official portal. Follow these simple steps to download it:

1. **Visit the Official Website:** Go to the official NTA CUET result portal at [cuet.nta.nic.in](https://cuet.nta.nic.in) or [exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-UG/](https://exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-UG/).
2. **Click on the Result Link:** Look for the active link titled **"CUET UG 2026 Scorecard / Result"** on the homepage.
3. **Enter Login Credentials:** Input your **Application Number** and **Date of Birth** (or Password).
4. **Enter Security Captcha:** Type the security PIN displayed on the screen.
5. **Submit & View:** Click on the "Submit" or "Login" button.
6. **Download & Print:** Your scorecard will be displayed on the screen. Save a PDF copy and take a few printouts for your admission file.

## What Details Will Be Mentioned on Your CUET Scorecard?

Your CUET UG scorecard is a vital document for university counseling. It contains crucial details that you must verify carefully:

- **Candidate Credentials:** Full Name, Roll Number, Application Number, Category, and Gender.
- **Subject-wise NTA Scores:** For each domain, language, and general test paper, your raw score and percentile score will be listed.
- **Qualifying Status:** Details indicating your eligibility for participating university counseling.
- **NTA Signature & Stamp:** Verification marks from the examination agency.

*Note: NTA only declares the scorecard. It does not release a common merit list or cutoff list. Individual universities prepare their own merit lists based on your CUET scores.*

## CUET UG 2026 Normalization Process: How Scores Are Calculated

In previous cycles, CUET used a complex equi-percentile normalization method due to exams being held across multiple shifts for the same subject. 

For the 2026 cycle, NTA conducted exams in a hybrid mode (both pen-and-paper and computer-based tests) and scheduled most major domain subjects in **single shifts**. As a result:
- **No Normalization for Single-Shift Exams:** For subjects conducted in a single shift, your raw scores are directly converted into percentile ranks.
- **Multi-Shift Subjects:** Normalization is only applied if a specific subject was conducted in multiple shifts to ensure no candidate is disadvantaged by a tougher paper set.

## What to Do After CUET UG Results?

Once your scorecard is in hand, the real admission process begins. Here is what you should prepare for:

### 1. Participate in University Counselling Portals
Each university conducts its own counseling process. You must apply separately on their portals:
- **Delhi University (DU):** Register on the Common Seat Allocation System (CSAS) portal using your CUET score.
- **Banaras Hindu University (BHU):** Apply on the BHU admission portal.
- **Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI) & Allahabad University:** Monitor their individual portals for cutoffs.

### 2. Compare Cutoffs and Course Combinations
Before choosing colleges, check last year's cutoffs for your preferred BBA, BCA, B.Com, or B.Sc. courses.

## FAQs

### When will the CUET UG 2026 result be declared?
The CUET UG 2026 results are expected to be declared by the National Testing Agency (NTA) in the first week of July 2026, tentatively around July 7, 2026.

### Where can I check my CUET UG 2026 scorecard?
Candidates can check and download their CUET UG scorecard on the official NTA portal at cuet.nta.nic.in or exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-UG/.

### What login credentials are required to check the CUET result?
You will need your CUET UG 2026 Application Number and Date of Birth (or Password) to access your scorecard on the portal.

### Does NTA release a common cutoff list for admissions?
No, NTA only declares the candidate scorecards. Individual participating universities will release their own cutoff marks and merit lists on their respective admission portals.

### Can I get admission to BBA or BCA without a CUET score?
Yes, you can get direct admission to UGC-approved Online BBA and Online BCA programs offered by top universities (like Amity, LPU, Jain, and Chandigarh University) based on your Class 12 marks, without appearing for CUET.
