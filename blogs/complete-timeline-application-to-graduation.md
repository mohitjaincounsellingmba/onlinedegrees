# The Complete Timeline: From Application to Graduation for Online MBA Students

*Introduce the importance of planning the MBA journey.*

## 1. Pre‑Application (Months –2 to –1)
- Research programs, check accreditation, attend webinars.
- Prepare GMAT/GRE, gather transcripts.
- Draft essays, request recommendation letters.

## 2. Application Period (Month 0)
- Submit online application.
- Follow up with interviews (if any).
- Secure funding / scholarships.

## 3. Admission & Enrollment (Month 1‑2)
- Receive admission decision.
- Accept offer, pay deposit.
- Register for orientation.

## 4. Orientation & Onboarding (Week 1)
- Platform walkthrough, meet cohort, set up learning tools.

## 5. Core Curriculum (Months 3‑12)
- Semester‑wise breakdown of core courses, assignments, exams.
- Mid‑semester check‑ins, networking events.

## 6. Specializations & Electives (Months 13‑24)
- Choose concentration, complete project work.
- Internship or capstone preparation.

## 7. Capstone / Thesis (Months 25‑30)
- Define research topic, work with advisor, submit final paper.

## 8. Graduation & Beyond (Month 31)
- Complete all credits, apply for graduation.
- Attend virtual commencement, receive diploma.
- Leverage alumni network for career moves.
