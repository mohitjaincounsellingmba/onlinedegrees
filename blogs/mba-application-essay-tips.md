# MBA Application Essay Tips That Got Me Accepted

*Intro: Briefly share personal experience of essay success.*

## 1. Understand the Prompt Deeply
- Break down each question.
- Align with program values.

## 2. Tell a Story, Not a List
- Use a clear narrative arc.
- Highlight personal growth.

## 3. Show, Don’t Just Say
- Provide concrete examples.
- Quantify impact where possible.

## 4. Connect to the Target School
- Reference specific faculty, labs, clubs.
- Explain why that school fits your goals.

## 5. Keep It Authentic
- Avoid buzzwords.
- Let your true voice shine.

## 6. Edit Ruthlessly
- Peer reviews, professional editing.
- Check word limits, grammar.
